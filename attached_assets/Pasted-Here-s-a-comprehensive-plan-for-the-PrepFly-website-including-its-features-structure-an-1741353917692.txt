Here's a **comprehensive plan** for the **PrepFly** website, including its **features, structure, and advanced functionalities** to ensure a seamless, engaging, and AI-powered test preparation platform.  

---

## **1. Website Overview**
**PrepFly** is an AI-powered test preparation platform designed to help users prepare for **IELTS, TOEFL, GRE, and other exams**. The website will provide study materials, AI-driven assessments, community support, and a seamless user experience.

---

## **2. Key Features & Functionalities**  

### **(A) Core Features**
✅ **AI-Powered Chatbot** – Provides instant answers, study suggestions, and assistance.  
✅ **Real-Time Speech Recognition** – Helps users practice speaking tests for IELTS & TOEFL.  
✅ **AI-Powered Essay Evaluation** – Provides feedback on writing tasks.  
✅ **Personalized Study Plans** – Users get tailored study schedules based on progress.  
✅ **Mock Tests & Quizzes** – Simulated tests for exam readiness.  
✅ **Live Classes & Webinars** – Online sessions with experts.  
✅ **Community Forum & Discussions** – Peer-to-peer support, moderated discussions.  
✅ **Downloadable Study Materials** – PDFs, notes, and video lessons.  
✅ **Multi-Language Support** – Translate content into various languages.  

---

### **(B) User Features**
👤 **User Profiles & Dashboard** – Track progress, manage subscriptions.  
🔑 **Social Media Login** – Google, Facebook, LinkedIn authentication.  
📩 **Newsletter Subscription** – Get updates on new courses & study tips.  
🕹️ **Interactive Learning Tools** – Flashcards, mind maps, concept videos.  

---

### **(C) Navigation & UI/UX**
📌 **Header & Navigation Bar** – Easy access to all sections.  
📱 **Mobile-Friendly Navigation Drawer** – Responsive sidebar for mobile users.  
🌙 **Dark Mode & Theme Customization** – User preference settings.  
⚡ **SEO-Optimized Pages** – Fast loading & structured metadata for rankings.  
📊 **Progress Tracking** – Dashboard with analytics & reports.  

---

### **(D) Pricing & Plans**
💰 **Freemium Model** – Free basic access, paid premium plans.  
✔ **Subscription Plans** – Monthly & yearly packages.  
🎁 **Discounts & Coupons** – Seasonal promotions.
